Polymarket Automated Trading Hub

An automated prediction-market trading hub with async data ingestion, market analysis, risk management, PostgreSQL persistence, and API-driven execution design.

GitHub Repository Live Demo

Case Study

Problem and solution

Challenge

Prediction-market trading requires structured data ingestion, probability assessment, real-time market monitoring, position sizing, and strong risk controls. Without this, automation becomes fragile and dangerous.


Approach

The system is designed as a modular backend: collectors pull market data, analytics services calculate edge, risk modules size positions, and execution services manage orders while persisting all state for review.


Key Features

  • Async trading infrastructure
  • Real-time market and order-book handling
  • Kelly Criterion-inspired position sizing
  • AI-assisted market summarization and decision support
  • PostgreSQL-backed state, metrics, and trade history
  • API-first structure for dashboards and bots

Architecture

  • FastAPI backend exposes trading, analytics, and health endpoints
  • WebSocket services process live market data
  • Risk engine controls exposure, sizing, and trade limits
  • Database stores markets, signals, trades, wallet state, and logs
